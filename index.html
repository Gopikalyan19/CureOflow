FAQ

How does the system help prevent drug shortages?
The system monitors inventory levels in real-time and sends alerts when stocks are low. It also generates automated supply orders to replenish inventory before running out.
Can the system track drug expiration dates?
Yes, it can track batch numbers and expiry dates, alerting you when drugs are approaching their expiration, ensuring proper stock usage.
What kind of reports does the cure O flow provide?
You can generate detailed reports on drug usage, vendor performance, stock levels, and procurement efficiency, as well as use predictive analytics to forecast future demand.
